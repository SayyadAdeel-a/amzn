# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Football fans, streetwear enthusiasts, and people looking for trendy, hype-driven apparel ahead of the 2026 World Cup. They are visually driven, trend-conscious, and appreciate high-end digital editorial experiences.

## Product Purpose
To sell FIFA 2026 gear, retro shirts, and streetwear through a highly engaging, trustworthy, and visually striking platform that feels more like a hypebeast editorial magazine than a standard e-commerce store.

## Brand Personality
Hype, modern, premium editorial, aggressive but clean.

## Anti-references
- Anything that looks like a standard PDF or Wikipedia article.
- Overly chaotic or messy "brutalist" designs.
- Generic, cheap-looking e-commerce templates.
- Boring, corporate SaaS templates.

## Design Principles
- **Editorial over E-commerce:** Treat every product and blog post like a magazine spread, prioritizing massive typography and striking layouts.
- **Hype is in the Details:** Use micro-interactions, sharp contrast, and purposeful neon accents against deep blacks.
- **Aggressive but Clean:** Use bold, tightly tracked display typography, but maintain readable, breathable body copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability (neon against black).
- Ensure reduced motion fallbacks for any intense animations.
